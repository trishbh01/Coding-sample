Data sources

dots1980_2008.dta: Bilateral trade data from the Direction of Trade Statistics (IMF) from 1960 to 2005, every 5 years.

dist_cepii.dta: CEPII dataset on bilateral distance and other trade costs. (www.cepii.fr)

RTA: Regional trade agreements from José de Sousa (http://jdesousa.univ.free.fr/data.htm)

CU: Currency unions from from José de Sousa (http://jdesousa.univ.free.fr/data.htm)

PWT: Penn World Tables 7.1.
